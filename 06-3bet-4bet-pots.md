3-bet pots: Preflop aggression creates nut advantage for aggressor, lower SPR = easier stack-offs.

## 3-Bet Pot Dynamics & SPR
**SPR Impact**: 3-bet pot ~20-25bb pot (100bb stacks) → SPR 4-5:1. Fewer streets, bigger bets.

**Aggressor Range**: Polarized/linear (QQ+/AK value, A5s/KQs bluffs). Stronger equity/nuts.<grok-card data-id="fcea62" data-type="citation_card"></grok-card>
- IP Aggressor (BTN 3-bet vs CO): C-bet 70-90% (even coordinated boards).<grok-card data-id="181ae3" data-type="citation_card"></grok-card>
- OOP Aggressor (BB 3-bet vs BTN): Still high c-bet 60-80% (position less important low SPR).<grok-card data-id="b2a0f9" data-type="citation_card"></grok-card>

**Sizing**: Smaller (20-40% pot) – low SPR allows river stack-offs.

## Aggressor Strategies in 3-Bet Pots
**Flop C-Betting**:
- Dry/High-card (AKT): 90-100% (range bet small, merged/polarized).
- Coordinated (987): 70-80% (protect, but check some overpairs IP).

**Value**: QQ+, sets, TPTK+.
**Bluffs**: Blockers (A/K), gutshots.

![3-Bet Flop C-Bet Chart](https://upswingpoker.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/gto-cbet-quiz.jpg)
*(Source: Upswing – 3-bet pot c-bet ranges)<grok-card data-id="2555aa" data-type="citation_card"></grok-card>

**Turn/River**: Barrel polarized (nuts/air), small sizes. Overbet rivers IP.

## Defender Strategies (Caller in 3-Bet Pot)
**OOP Defender (e.g., CO vs BTN 3-bet)**:
- Check-Raise: 15-25% (sets, combo draws) – higher than SRP.
- Check-Call: Medium pairs/draws (MDF ~40-50%).
- Check-Fold: Air (tighter defense low SPR).

**IP Defender**: Call more, raise less (leverage position).

## 4-Bet Pots: Polarized & Low SPR
**Preflop Ranges** (100bb 6-Max):
- 3-Bet: 8-12% (QQ+/AK value, suited Ax bluffs).
- 4-Bet: 3-5% polarized (KK+/AA value, A5s bluffs). Value:Bluff ~2:1.

| Position | 3-Bet Freq | 4-Bet Value | 4-Bet Bluff |
|----------|------------|-------------|-------------|
| **BTN vs CO** | 10% | KK+/AA | A5s-A2s |
| **BB vs BTN** | 12% | QQ+/AK | KQs, A4s<grok-card data-id="469b5e" data-type="citation_card"></grok-card> |

## Key Takeaways
- **3-Bet Pots**: Aggressor c-bets high freq/small size (nut advantage). Defend polarized OOP.
- **Low SPR**: Stack-off easier, protect more aggressively.
- **4-Bet Pots**: Polarized preflop/postflop, small donks OOP.
- Practice: GTO Wizard trainer for 3/4-bet flops.
- Exploit: Vs passive callers → value thin; vs aggro 3-bettors → 4-bet light.
